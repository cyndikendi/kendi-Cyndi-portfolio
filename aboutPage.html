Cyndi Kendi

Web, App & Unity Game Developer

Hi! I'm Cyndi, a passionate developer and creative problem solver from Kenya. My journey in tech began with curiosity and a love for games, which soon grew into a full-blown career in software and game development. I thrive on turning ideas into interactive experiences—whether that's a fun mobile app, a beautiful website, or an immersive Unity game.

I believe technology should be both useful and delightful. My work is guided by a desire to make a positive impact, empower others, and keep learning every day. I value teamwork, honesty, and a growth mindset. I enjoy collaborating with people from diverse backgrounds and always look for ways to make my projects more inclusive and accessible.

Outside of coding, I love exploring new recipes, listening to music, and spending time outdoors—especially swimming and cycling. I’m also a big fan of movies and am always up for a skating challenge (even if I’m still learning!).

My goal is to keep growing as a developer, contribute to meaningful projects, and inspire others—especially young women—to pursue their dreams in tech. If you’d like to connect, collaborate, or just say hi, feel free to reach out!
